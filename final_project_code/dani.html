From Mexico to the States I came,
A Latina with a unique name,
Raised in a land so far away,
But here I found my place to stay.

My dreams were once to teach the young,

I followed my heart, not others' voices,
And found a life filled with choices.

In Chicago, I found a friend,

I found the strength to take my stride,
To live my life the way I choose,

About Us

Welcome to Kyoto Japanese Hibachi Express, the premier hibachi restaurant in Tontitown, Arkansas. We are passionate about providing our guests with an extraordinary dining experience that is both entertaining and delicious. Our skilled chefs bring a unique blend of Japanese culinary expertise and showmanship to every meal, creating a thrilling hibachi performance right at your table. From the dazzling knife skills to the mesmerizing fire tricks, our chefs are sure to leave you in awe.
